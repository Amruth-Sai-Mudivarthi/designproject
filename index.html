Images

Here are some of the rice leaf disease images with description about them and also the remedies used

Rice Leaf Blast

This disease is caused by the fungus Magnaporthe oryzae. It can cause significant yield losses in rice crops.

Cure: Use resistant varieties and fungicides. Maintain proper field sanitation.

Bacterial Blight

Bacterial blight is caused by Xanthomonas oryzae. It leads to yellowing and wilting of rice plants.

Cure: Use resistant varieties and proper fertilization. Avoid planting in areas with a history of blight.

Brown Spot

Brown spot is caused by the fungus Bipolaris oryzae. It appears as small, round to oval, brown spots on leaves, leading to reduced photosynthesis and grain yield.

Cure: Apply balanced fertilization, especially with potassium, and use fungicides if necessary. Ensure proper field drainage and avoid excessive irrigation.
